Book

Against the Dao: How Xi Jinping’s Rhetoric of Control Is Unraveling China

This book examines Xi Jinping’s governance through the lens of Daoist philosophy, particularly Laozi’s concept of Wuwei. It argues that excessive control, ideological rigidity, and symbolic politics have produced profound consequences for China’s political and economic development.
